Mission Statement for the Blog

Tue 18 March 2014
by Aaron Fisher
in Blog

tagged blog, goals

In this blog, I'll talk about issues in statistics from a graduate student's perspective. Some specific topics include: surviving a PhD, enjoying a PhD, designing intuitive graphics, and dealing with high dimensional data. I'll also rant from time to time about food/cooking, and culture in general.

I'll try to keeps these posts clear and concise, with working code examples.

Hope you enjoy!
